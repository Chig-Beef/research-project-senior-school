General information

Differences

There are many differences between Middle School and Senior School, such as:

  1. There is no bell in senior school, you are expected to arrive on classes on time by your own accord.

Room numbers

When teachers tell you where a classroom is, they may say something like "3F17".

The 3 refers to the block number, so block 3.

The F tells you whether it is ground level (G) or on the first floor (F).

The last two numbers are just the room code, so you have to look at the number on each classroom's door to find the correct one.

The rooms are usually in order.

If the specific room number isn't given for a teacher, you can find their office in Senior School Student Services.
